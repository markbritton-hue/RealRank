League Rules

Tournament Rules

  1. All live wells must be checked before the tournament starts.
  2. All boats MUST pass by the start boat before heading to the fishing area.
  3. No live bait. Artificial lures only.
  4. Culling must be done before leaving the water. The courtesy bump process allows handlers to check fish on an official board before weighing. Once weigh-in staff takes possession, everything becomes official. You can no longer handle/touch your fish until given back to you. Short fish penalties apply.
  5. Maximum five bass at weigh-in. Exceeding this results in automatic disqualification.
  6. ALL fish MUST be transported to the weigh-in scales in a bag with adequate water and returned to the lake immediately after weigh-in. Bass must be handled carefully; rough treatment is prohibited.
  7. A 100-foot perimeter must be allowed to other boaters. Respect other anglers' space on the water.
  8. Standings are determined by total weight.
  9. Ties are broken by the heaviest individual bass.
  10. Competitors must follow all state and local fishing laws. Violations result in disqualification.
  11. No pre-tournament fishing allowed on event day.

⚠️ Penalties

🐟
Dead Fish
Half-pound deduction per fish
🚤
Improper Transport
Half-pound deduction per fish
📏
Short Fish
Disqualification of the undersized fish plus removal of heaviest bass weight

🏆 Classic Tournament Qualification

  • • Top 10 points-qualified teams plus two wild-card teams qualify (directors excluded).
  • • A minimum of eight regular season tournaments is required to qualify.
  • • No substitutions are allowed in the Classic.
  • • Wild cards are determined by drawing from remaining teams after the top 10 are finalized.
